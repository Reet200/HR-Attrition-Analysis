# HR-Attrition-Analysis
Employee attrition analysis using SQL, Python and Power BI on IBM HR dataset
# HR Employee Attrition Analysis
## 🔧 Tools Used
- MySQL — Data querying and business analysis
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn) — Data cleaning and EDA
- Power BI — Interactive dashboard

## 📊 Dataset
- IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition Dataset
- 1,470 employee records | 35 columns
- Source: Kaggle

## 🎯 Project Objective
Analyze employee attrition patterns to identify key factors driving 
employee turnover and provide actionable HR retention recommendations.

## 🔍 Key Findings
- Overall attrition rate is 16.12% (237 out of 1,470 employees)
- Sales Representatives have highest attrition rate (40%+)
- Employees doing overtime are 2x more likely to leave
- Frequent business travelers show significantly higher attrition
- Under 25 age group has highest attrition — young employees job hop more
- Lower monthly income strongly correlates with higher attrition

## 💡 Business Recommendation
Regardless of job role, the three strongest predictors of attrition 
are overtime, frequent travel, and low compensation. HR should 
prioritize overtime policies, travel allowances, and salary revision 
specifically for Sales roles to improve retention.
